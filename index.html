About Us

Shining Light into Young Lives

Nuru ya Uzima (Light of Life) is a Christian nonprofit organization committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable children and youth through love, compassion, and empowerment. We walk alongside those abandoned, neglected, or orphaned—restoring their dignity and helping them rediscover their identity and purpose.

Through holistic care rooted in faith and community values, we aim to break the cycles of despair and help children become powerful agents of positive change in their homes and societies.

  • ✔ Advocate for children’s rights and dignity
  • ✔ Support education and reintegration into society
  • ✔ Promote spiritual, emotional, and mental healing
  • ✔ Empower youths to become change-makers
  • ✔ Create spaces for growth and expression
Empowering Lives

Our Programs

Healing and Restoration

Safe spaces, spiritual mentorship, and psychosocial support to heal trauma and restore dignity to vulnerable children and youth.

Education & Reintegration

Supporting access to education and reintegration into families or communities for lasting impact and inclusion.

Youth Camps & Empowerment

Organizing faith-filled camps and workshops that equip youth with leadership skills, purpose, and confidence to transform communities.
